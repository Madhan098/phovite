Help Center

Find answers to common questions about PhoVite

How does PhoVite work?

PhoVite uses advanced AI technology to transform your personal photos into professional event invitations. Simply upload a photo, choose your event type and style (vibe), provide event details, and our AI will generate a stunning invitation in seconds. You can then download and share it with your guests.

What types of photos work best?

For best results, use clear, well-lit photos with the main subject (person or scene) prominently featured. Portrait photos work great for personal events like birthdays and weddings. Landscape or group photos can work well for parties and corporate events. Avoid blurry or heavily filtered images.

What are "Vibes" and how do I choose one?

Vibes are style presets that determine the aesthetic of your invitation. Choose Neon for energetic, modern events; Royal for elegant, luxurious occasions; Minimal for clean, contemporary designs; or Floral for romantic, nature-inspired themes.

How do I download my invitation?

After your invitation is generated, click the "Download PhoVite" button. The image will be saved to your device in high-quality PNG format (16:9 aspect ratio), perfect for sharing via email, social media, or messaging apps. If you're logged in, your invitations are also saved to your Dashboard for future access.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We take privacy seriously. Your photos are processed securely and are never shared with third parties for marketing purposes. All passwords are encrypted, and your generated invitations are only accessible through your account. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I regenerate an invitation if I don't like it?

Yes! Click the "Create Another" button to return to the form and generate a new invitation with different settings. You can try different vibes, photos, or event details until you get the perfect result.

How long does generation take?

Most invitations are generated in 30-60 seconds. The AI needs time to analyze your photo, generate creative text, and render the final design. Complex images or high server load may occasionally take a bit longer.
