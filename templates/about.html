About PhoVite

We're revolutionizing event invitations by combining personal photography with cutting-edge AI technology.

Our Story

PhoVite was born from a simple idea: your special moments deserve invitations that are as unique as the events themselves. Traditional invitation makers force you to choose from generic templates, while custom design services are expensive and time-consuming.

We asked ourselves: what if AI could transform your personal photos into stunning, professional-grade invitations in seconds? That's exactly what we built.

Using state-of-the-art generative AI models, PhoVite seamlessly blends your photos with artistic backgrounds, intelligent text generation, and customizable vibes to create invitations that truly capture the essence of your celebration.
