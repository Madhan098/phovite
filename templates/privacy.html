Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use PhoVite, we collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email address, password)
  • Photos you upload for invitation generation
  • Event details and preferences you provide
  • Generated invitations and their metadata

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate AI-powered invitations based on your photos and preferences
  • Maintain and improve our service
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our platform

3. Your Photos and AI Processing

Your photos are processed using third-party AI services (Google Gemini and Replicate) to generate invitations. We do not sell your photos to third parties. Photos are stored securely and are only accessible to you through your account.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Your password is hashed using secure algorithms, and all data transmission is encrypted.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your generated invitations
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Gemini AI for text generation
  • Replicate for image generation and processing
  • Uploadcare for file uploads

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@phovite.ai.
